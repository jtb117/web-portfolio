About me

I have a passion for researching problems and analyzing results. As I got involved in neuroscience research, I found myself encountering the power of computer science tools over and over again. From there, my academic interests broadened to incorporate these. The bredth of study I have pursued is ultimately tied together by Data Science. I've come to learn that, across all disciplines, data is waiting to be analyzed.

I'm currently working as a Software Engineer at DataDock Solutions. On the side, I'm building a tool for the popular messaging platform GroupMe which serves as a bot providing analysis, charts, and other information to users.

Oppurtunities to apply my experience and develop further are always welcome.
